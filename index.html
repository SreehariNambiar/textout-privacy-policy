Last updated: March 23, 2026

Welcome to Textout, a product of Pixelera Technologies LLP, a company based in India. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you register and use Textout:

  • Full Name – provided during account registration
  • Email Address – used for account login and communication
  • Phone Number – collected during account setup
  • Profile Photo – uploaded voluntarily by you
  • User-Generated Content – messages, media, and content you create within the app
  • Device Information – device type, OS version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data – app interactions and feature usage collected via analytics tools
  • Location Data – approximate or precise location, only when you grant permission
  • Payment Information – transaction data processed through Google Play Billing (we do not store card details)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your Textout account
  • Enable chat, messaging, and real-time communication features
  • Send push notifications relevant to your activity
  • Process in-app purchases via Google Play Billing
  • Analyse app performance and improve user experience using PostHog and Sentry
  • Monitor and fix errors and crashes (via Sentry)
  • Comply with legal obligations under applicable Indian law

3. Third-Party Services

Textout integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) – authentication, push notifications, and cloud storage. Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing – in-app purchases and subscription management. Privacy Policy
  • PostHog – product analytics to understand how users interact with Textout. Privacy Policy
  • Sentry – error tracking and crash reporting to improve app stability. Privacy Policy

4. Permissions We Request

Textout may request the following device permissions:

  • Camera – to capture photos for profile pictures or content
  • Storage / Media – to read and save images, videos, and audio files
  • Push Notifications – to send you alerts, messages, and updates
  • Location – to provide location-based features (only when permitted)

You can revoke any of these permissions at any time from your device settings.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted through Firebase (Google Cloud). We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You may delete your account and all associated data directly from within the app by going to Settings → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account". Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately. Alternatively, you may contact us at pixeleratech@gmail.com and we will process your request within 30 days in accordance with applicable Indian data protection laws.
